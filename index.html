About me

Hi, I’m Kirra! I earned my degree in Computer Science from the University of North Florida and am currently based in Jacksonville, though I’m open to relocating. My web development skills include HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, PHP, React, Node.js, Angular, Bootstrap, and working with most SQL-based DBMSs. I’m also experienced with tools like WordPress, Odoo, Shopify, Figma, and most Adobe softwares. When I’m not building websites or crafting user interfaces, you’ll likely find me surfing or binging movies from my letterboxd watchlist!
